This course has been designed to help you better understand and manage the powerful ever-present role that emotions play in communication and conflict. Learn actionable strategies on how to achieve a harmonious work environment.

Are you aware of your emotions when talking to people, or do they tend to sneak up on you?

<Blockquote>
  Do you get emotional when communicating?
  Do you normally engage with people from a positive or negative state?
</Blockquote>

Emotions have a constant impact in communication, and in conflict. By recognizing emotions, and delving into the root causes of both positive and negative emotions, we can gain insight into effectively managing them for a more harmonious and productive work environment. Learn actionable skills on how to generate more positive emotions and achieve more balance in your life, and in the workplace.
